Permanent Driving License Procedure
Following are the stages in Application Submission in Issuing Driving Licence in the following order:
  1. Fill Applicant Details
  2. Make online payment
  3. Your Acknowledgement, Form 1A (Medical Form), Form 1 & appointement slip will be sent on your registered mail Id
  4. Get signature done by a doctor (if required) on your Medical form (form 1A) and resend us via email or whatsapp
  5. DL Test Slot Booking (applicable for only some states)
  6. Visit RTO on your appointment date, attend your test, and get your Driving Licence
