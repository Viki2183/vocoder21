Regional Transport Office (RTO)

You would have heard a lot about Regional Transport Office but do you really know what RTO stands for and what are the exact functions of Regional Transport Office? Do you know the full-form of RTO and do you know the address of RTO? Well, in this article, we have covered all this information about RTO. We will first talk about a brief introduction to RTO and then we will move ahead to other points.

To begin with, RTO stands for Regional Transport Office and these are basically the regional offices that fall under Motor vehicle Departments. These departments were established under Motor Vehicles Act of 1988 and as per the act, the RTOs were setup across the nation. There are certain activities and functions assigned to RTO and they are common for all the Regional Transport offices.

As of now, each state and each union territory hasÂ Regional Transport OfficeÂ and most of the states have many R.T.O. ideally, there is a different RTO for each district which performs the duties that are defined by the Motor Vehicle Act of 1988.

 

What is Temporary RTO Registration for Vehicles?

You would have noticed a Temp written on the number plates of some of the vehicles. Do you know what does that stands for? Well, that stands for Temporary and it means that the registration number on the vehicle is temporary. Now you must be wondering that what this temporary registration is.

Previously, the temporary registration numbers were given to vehicles so that they could have been transported from the dealer to the place where they are supposed to be registered (RTO Office). This temporary number plate is usually valid for 7 days in case of the car but for the chassis where there is a need to build the body, the duration is 30 days. In this period of time, the owner can complete the built of the body and then he can get the vehicle registered by visiting the Nearest RTO Office.

As of now, there are not many cases where you would see the Temp number plate as the dealers now have the power to register the vehicle. They can submit the vehicle’s paper to theÂ Regional Transport OfficeÂ and they can assign you a number. In most of the cases, the vehicles come with a number pre-assigned to them.

What are the Functions of the Regional Transport Office?

There are three basic functions that are assigned to every RTO. Knowing about these functions can help you in getting a brief understanding of what does a Regional Transport Office does. In addition to this, we will talk about the activities performed by RTO in the later stages once you have an idea about the functions of the Regional Transport Office.

The three basic functions of theÂ Regional Transport OfficeÂ are

  • To ensure coordinated development of road transportation by the management of the permits issues.
  • To enforce the provisions of the various acts which are a part of the Motor Vehicle Act and to ensure the implementation of Motor Vehicles Rules that are applicable on the state level as well as on the central level. The department needs to stay in sync with the government of India.
  • The RTO also needs to collect the charges and the taxes as per the provision of the vehicle act and the various provisions of the vehicle act.

This was all about the functions of RTO and we will now talk about the activities of Regional Transport Office.

What are the Activities performed by Regional Transport Office?

You must not be confused between the activities and functions of RTO as the activities ofÂ Regional Transport OfficeÂ are the subset of functions. If you wish to know about the activities that are performed by RTO then you can check out the list of activities available below.

  • Registration of New Vehicle for personal use as well as commercial use.
  • Registration of Vehicle after the transfer of ownership or registration of mortgage transactions after hypothecation.
  • Issuance of License for the public transits as well as freight
  • Issuing the Learners and Permanent Driving License after conducting the test for the applicant.
  • Issuing of the badges to the commercial drivers along with the commercial driving license.
  • Issuance of International Driving License.
  • Maintenance of database for the license issued.
  • Maintenance of the database for the vehicles registered in an Regional Transport Office.
  • Collection of Motor Vehicle Tax and Road Tax
  • Checking the validity of Insurance as well as PUC Certificate of Vehicles.
  • Inspection of Vehicles and certifying the fitness of the transport vehicles.

What is the process to check your vehicle registration details via RTO Online method? (Vehicle Information)

With the increasing dependency on technology, there are many facilities that are made available for the people through online medium. Today, it is possible to check the RTO Vehicle Information and registration status of the vehicle online and all you need is the registration number of the vehicle.

Follow the steps below to check the RTO Vehicle Information and vehicle registration status details online.

  • You need to visit the Vahan Portal to begin the search and the link to the portal is https://vahan.nic.in
  • On this portal, you will notice an options menu on left side of the page. From the options displayed, click on Vehicle Status. You will now be taken to a new page for searching the vehicle records. If you do not find the link then you can directly click on https://vahan.nic.in/nrservices/faces/user/jsp/SearchStatus.jsp
  • Now, enter the Vehicle Number along with the Captcha Code and click on search button. The RTO Registration?status of the vehicle will now be displayed.
  • The details that will be displayed would include the Regional Transport Office where the vehicle is registered, Chassis Number, Engine Number, Vehicle Class and also the NOC Details if applicable.

This way, you can check if the vehicle has been registered or not along with other RTO Vehicle Information that you might need. All these details can be obtained online and in case of discrepancy, you should visit Nearest RTO Office.

Is it possible to get a fancy number for your vehicle?

The number of the car is pre-assigned by the dealer but you can choose a fancy number as per your wish. Below is the process for the same.

  • You can select the number that you want for your car and the information about the available number is available via RTO online Refer to the link below to get the information RTO Online – https://parivahan.gov.in/fancy/faces/public/login.xhtml
  • Now, visit the RTO Office?along with Aadhaar card, proof of payment of booking amount, form for the fancy Ensure that the documents are self-attested and they are completely filled.
  • Go to the counter and check for the availability. If the number is available, the officer will approve the number and you will then have to visit the DO for approval.
  • In the later stages, you can make the payment collect the fancy number allotment letter. Once that is done, you can get the number for your car or bike.
